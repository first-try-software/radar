Inspired by ship's radar on the bridge—the sweeping arm locates and tracks projects. The health indicators appear as "blips" detected by the scan.

B1: Radar Sweep ⭐ Animated sweep detects project status
B2: Static Radar For print and static applications
B3: Minimal Radar Clean, focused design
B4: Status Rings Rings indicate health zones

Recommendation

B3 (Minimal Radar) or C3 (Target Reticle) best balance:

  • The "zeroing in" concept you mentioned
  • Connection to The Bridge's nautical/navigation theme
  • Clear visibility of the three health status shapes
