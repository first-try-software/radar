Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

  • Use the full-color version on dark backgrounds
  • Maintain the correct aspect ratio
  • Use monochrome version when color isn't available
  • Keep adequate clear space around the logo
  • Use the favicon version at small sizes

✗ Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change the colors of individual shapes
  • Add drop shadows or effects
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Rotate or skew the logo
